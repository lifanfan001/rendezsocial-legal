Child Safety Standards

Rendez is an adult-only dating application intended exclusively for users aged 18 and above.

We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE/CSAM).

Our Measures

  • Age-gating during registration. Users must confirm they are at least 18 years old.
  • Prohibition of any content involving minors.
  • In-app mechanisms for reporting and blocking users.
  • Review of reported accounts and content.
  • Account suspension and cooperation with law enforcement when required by law.
