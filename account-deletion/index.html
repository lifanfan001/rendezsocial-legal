Account Deletion

Request to delete your Rendez account and data

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Rendez account, please send an email request to our support team. You do not need to log in to your account to make this request.

Email Address:

contact.rendez@gmail.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted from our systems:

  • User account and profile information (name, email, date of birth, gender, height, location preferences)
  • Profile photos and uploaded images
  • Event invitations you created
  • Applications you submitted to events
  • Matches and connections with other users
  • Chat messages and conversation history
  • User preferences and settings
  • Analytics identifiers linked to your account
  • App usage data associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for the following reasons:

  • Legal or Regulatory Compliance: We may retain certain information as required by law, court orders, or regulatory requirements.
  • Fraud Prevention and Security: Information related to security incidents, fraud prevention, or abuse may be retained to protect our services and users.
  • Transaction or Audit Logs: Anonymized transaction records and audit logs may be retained for business operations and compliance purposes.

Retention Period: Any retained data will be permanently deleted within 90 days, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Processing Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 to 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email at the address you used to register your account once the deletion has been completed.

Email Template

You can copy and paste the following template into your email:

Subject: Account Deletion Request To: contact.rendez@gmail.com Dear Rendez Support Team, I am writing to request the permanent deletion of my Rendez account and all associated data. Registered Email: [Your email address] User ID: [Your User ID, if available] I understand that this action is irreversible and that my account and data will be permanently deleted in accordance with your account deletion policy. Thank you for your assistance. Sincerely, [Your name]

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I request deletion?

After you send your deletion request, our support team will verify your identity and process the request. Your account will be deactivated and scheduled for deletion. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete, typically within 7 to 30 days.

Can I recover my account?

No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account and data have been deleted, they cannot be recovered. If you wish to use Rendez again in the future, you will need to create a new account.

What if I no longer have access to my email?

If you no longer have access to the email address associated with your account, please contact us at contact.rendez@gmail.com with as much information as possible to verify your identity. We may ask for additional verification to ensure account security.
