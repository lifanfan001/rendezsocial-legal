Rendez – Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-01

This Cookie Policy explains how Rendez (“Rendez”, “we”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our Service.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve user experience and provide information to website owners.

In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies such as local storage, software development kits (SDKs) in our mobile app, and pixels or tags for analytics and measurement.

2. How We Use Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings;
  • Keep you signed in and help with account authentication;
  • Understand how you use our website or app;
  • Improve performance, security and reliability of the Service.

Some cookies and similar technologies are essential for the Service to function properly, while others are optional and used for analytics and improvement.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Strictly necessary cookies: required for core features such as logging in, maintaining your session and navigating between pages.
  • Performance and analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors use our Service (for example, which screens are most popular or where users encounter errors) so that we can improve stability and usability.
  • Functionality cookies: allow us to remember your preferences (such as language or region) and provide a more personalized experience.

At this stage of Rendez, our use of cookies and similar technologies is limited and mainly related to technical operation, authentication and basic analytics (including tools such as Firebase and other usage analytics services).

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Each browser is different, so please check your browser's help menu to learn how to change cookies settings.

If you disable certain cookies, some parts of the Service may not function properly or may become less responsive.

On mobile devices, you can often manage app tracking, advertising and analytics preferences through your device settings (for example, settings related to ad tracking or app-specific permissions).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
